President's Message

Dear Colleagues,

The Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN) has been the official representative body for Pakistani neurologists for nearly three decades, committed to fostering connections, promoting excellence, and advancing the field of neurology in our country.

The Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN) proudly invites all eligible neurologists with postgraduate qualification in Neurology to register for PSN Membership and inclusion in the PSN Digital Directory. This initiative aims to further strengthen our professional network and enhance advocacy for the field of neurology in Pakistan.

Professor Naila N. Shahbaz
President, Pakistan Society of Neurology

Eligibility Criteria

  • FCPS Neurology / MD Neurology / Diplomat American Board / MRCP (UK) with Neurology CCT
  • Currently practicing Clinical Neurology in Pakistan
  • Valid PMDC registration

Membership Information

The PSN Digital Directory will serve as a comprehensive database of qualified neurologists in Pakistan, facilitating:

  • Professional networking and collaboration
  • Access to continuing medical education opportunities
  • Participation in research initiatives
  • Advocacy for neurology care in Pakistan

Join us in strengthening the neurology community in Pakistan.
