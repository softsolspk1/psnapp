President's Message

Dear Colleagues,
Assalam o Alaikum.

The Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN) has been the official representative body for Pakistani neurologists for nearly three decades, committed to fostering connections, promoting excellence, and advancing the field of neurology in our country. The Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN) proudly invites all eligible neurologists with postgraduate qualification in Neurology to register for PSN Membership and inclusion in the PSN Digital Directory. This initiative aims to further strengthen our professional network and enhance advocacy for the field of neurology in Pakistan.

Profound Regards,
Professor Naila N. Shahbaz
