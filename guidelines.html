Guidelines Development Process

Our guidelines are developed through a rigorous process involving expert panels, literature review, and stakeholder consultation.

Guidelines Development Process
Literature Review

Comprehensive review of current research and international guidelines.

Expert Panel Formation

Assembly of subject matter experts and stakeholders.

Draft Development

Creation of initial guidelines based on evidence and expert consensus.

Stakeholder Review

Feedback collection from practitioners and stakeholders.

Final Approval

Review and approval by PSN Executive Committee.
