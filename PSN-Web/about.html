Welcome to Pakistan Society of Neurology

Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN) is the official representative body of Pakistani Neurologists working for almost three decades.

Who We Are

Pakistan neurology community held its first formal meeting in 1994 at Lahore while in 2003 organized its first national meeting under the umbrella of Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN). In March 2014, it formally got registered under the society act and became formally affiliated with the World Federation of Neurology (WFN) as an affiliated national representative body.

PSN primarily encourages neurologists within Pakistan to maintain links with each other, in order to:

  • Provide leadership to the existing and upcoming neurologists in Pakistan
  • Promote more effectively the welfare and interests of its members
  • Establish mutually beneficial relationships with other similar societies
  • Provide administrative infrastructure for academic neurology development
  • Exchange ideas to promote better patient care and enhance research activities

Our Core Values

Community

We work together as neurology professionals to accomplish the mission and vision of the society and to share our knowledge and skills in belonging to and serving the neurology community.

Leadership

We empower members, patients, and other stakeholders to make constructive changes in their own lives and the lives of others in the neurology community.

Integrity

We set and maintain the highest ethical standards for ourselves and our programs.

Respect

We embrace the self-respect and uniqueness of every person, being sensitive and empathetic to the needs of others.

Our Commitment to Excellence

Professional Development

Continuous learning and skill enhancement opportunities for neurologists

Collaboration

Building strong partnerships with national and international organizations

Research Excellence

Promoting and supporting neurological research in Pakistan

About PSN

Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN) is dedicated to promoting excellence in clinical neurology, research, and education while fostering collaboration among neurologists across Pakistan.
