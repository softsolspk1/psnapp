Research Ethics

All research projects must comply with PSN's ethical guidelines and obtain necessary approvals.

Research Ethics Guidelines
Institutional Review Board (IRB) Approval

All research involving human subjects must obtain IRB approval before commencement.

Informed Consent

Detailed guidelines for obtaining and documenting informed consent from research participants.

Data Protection

Requirements for secure handling and storage of research data and participant information.

Publication Ethics

Guidelines for authorship, conflict of interest disclosure, and responsible reporting of research findings.
