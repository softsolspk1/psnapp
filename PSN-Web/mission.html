Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to neurological excellence

Our Mission

To uphold the quality based patient-centered neurologic care and enhance member career satisfaction.

Deliver exceptional patient care
Support professional growth
Advance neurological research

Our Vision

To be essential to our members and lead the way in neurological excellence.

Foster innovation in neurology
Build strong professional community
Set standards in neurological care

Our Approach

Professionalism

We work collaboratively as a committed team to share our expertise and improve the quality of neurological care.

Education

We promote continuous learning and professional development through workshops, conferences, and training programs.

Collaboration

We foster partnerships with national and international organizations to advance neurological care in Pakistan.

About PSN

Pakistan Society of Neurology (PSN) is dedicated to promoting excellence in clinical neurology, research, and education while fostering collaboration among neurologists across Pakistan.
